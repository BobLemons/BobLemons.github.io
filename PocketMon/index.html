🎴 Pokémon TCG Drop Alert Bot

Personal project · single-user notification tool

This is a personal, non-commercial tool built and operated by one individual to monitor retailer websites and a handful of social media sources for new Pokémon Trading Card Game stock and restocks.

What it does

The bot periodically checks public product pages (Pokémon Center, Target, Walmart, GameStop, Best Buy, Costco, Sam's Club, TCGPlayer) and public posts on Reddit/Twitter/Facebook/Instagram/TikTok for new Pokémon TCG listings or restock mentions. When something new is found, it sends a single automated text message and email to the owner's own phone number and inbox — nothing more.

Who receives messages

Exactly one person: the owner of this tool, who configured their own phone number and email address to receive these alerts. No other phone numbers are messaged, no lists are purchased or sold, and no marketing of any kind is sent.

Message frequency & content

Messages are sent only when a new product or post is detected — typically a few times a day at most. Each message contains the source, a short product/post name, and a link. Reply STOP to opt out at any time (this immediately and permanently stops all future messages to that number).

Data & privacy

This tool does not collect, store, or share personal data about anyone other than the configuration the owner entered for their own use. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

The Restock Club

A small, friendly corner of the internet for Pokémon TCG fans tired of empty shelves and marked-up listings.

Why this exists

Anyone who's tried to grab a new set knows the drill: shelves get cleared in minutes, usually by automated checkout bots, and the same product reappears online an hour later at two or three times the price. That's frustrating for collectors, and especially rough for parents just trying to find a booster box for a kid who loves the game.

What we're about

  • No markups — the idea is to pay it forward, not profit off a shortage
  • No mystery middlemen — this is one person doing this in good faith
  • No purchases happen on this page (yet) — this is just the "here's what we're about" page

What's next

Right now this is just an awareness page — there's no store, checkout, or payment processing here. If a small, at-cost way to help local fans get product ever comes together, it'll be announced here first.
